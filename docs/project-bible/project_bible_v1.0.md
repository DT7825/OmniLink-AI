# 1. Purpose

# 2. Vision

To build an intelligent AI-powered humanitarian coordination platform that connects citizens, volunteers, NGOs, hospitals, government agencies, and emergency responders through a unified digital ecosystem.

---

# 3. Mission

# 8. Business Problem

Emergency response ecosystems often suffer from fragmented communication, delayed coordination, limited resource visibility, and inefficient volunteer management.

Organizations frequently rely on disconnected systems, manual processes, and incomplete information, resulting in slower response times and reduced operational efficiency.

OmniLink AI addresses these challenges by providing a unified digital platform for coordination, decision support, and intelligent automation.
